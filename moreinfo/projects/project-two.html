All About Me

Software Development Journey

I first became interested in software development in September 2022. I came across a post at work about applying for the Amazon Technical Academy. Unfortunately, I did not make it past the final phase. However, I came across Correlation One at our Career Choice Fair and immediately applied. I love the course so far! I like how I have a teaching assistant (TA) and a Career Success Coach to help guide me on my journey. So far I have learned HTML and am currently learning about CSS. I can't wait to complete this course, start applying for jobs, and start my career. What I find most interesting is that as a web developer is that I will always learn something new and that being hands-on will help me along the way.
